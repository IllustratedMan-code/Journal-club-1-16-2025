Gene Regulatory Networks

Bacillus subtilis

The paper wants to model the global transcriptional network inside of Bacillus subtilis. Bacillus subtilis is a gram positive bacteria. Gram positive bacteria have thick cell walls made of peptoglican. Bacillus subtilis is one of the most well studied bacteria so it made a good target for this paper.
Specifically, the paper tries to model the Bacillus subtilis undergoes a process called sporulation when under environmental stress. It is a last-ditch survival mechanism when no resources or adverse environmental conditions are present. Because it is easy to trigger, this is one of the most studied pathways. Spores are able to survive extreme conditions without nutrients indefinitely. Regulated by sigma factors.

Subtiwiki compiles all the interactions known about Bacillus subtilis into an easily accessible database. This paper uses subti-wiki to form the prior network that we will cover later.
