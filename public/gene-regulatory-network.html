What is a Gene Regulatory Network?

A gene regulatory network, or gene transcription network shows the relationship between proteins called transcription factors and the transcription of genes.
A simple transcriptional regulatory network. Sigma factors are analogous to general transcription factors found in eukaryotes, particularly TFIIB (which aids in assembly of the pre-initiation complex). Arrows show an activating/enhancing effect the perpendicular lines show an inhibitory/repressive effect.
